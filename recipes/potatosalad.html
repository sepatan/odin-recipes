Estonian Potato Salad

A fresh and filling potato salad for any occasion!

This will be a hit at any get together for its fresh and delicious taste, this is the traditional Estonian take on potato salad!

Ingredients

  • 4 - 5 boiled potatoes
  • 2 medium-sized boiled potatoes
  • 1 boiled egg
  • 100 g of boiled sausage
  • 1 apple
  • 1/2 cucumber
  • 100 g of peas
  • 200 g of sour cream
  • 200 g of mild mayonnaise
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Step by Step Directions

  1. Boil potatoes and carrots in salted water
  2. Peel potatoes and chop into fine cubes after cooldown
  3. chop carrots, apples, sausage into fine cubes
  4. mix all together and add peas
  5. mix in sour cream and mayonnaise together
  6. season with salt and pepper to taste
  7. let sit for a couple of hours
